WhatsApp Number — Kya karna chahiye?
Pehle kya ho raha tha: App number ko as-is store karta tha. Agar 9876543210 daalein to wa.me/9876543210 banta tha — jo unreliable hai.

Ab kya fix kiya:

10 digit number bina +91 daalein (e.g. 9876543210)
App automatically 91 prefix add kar dega wa.me link mein → wa.me/919876543210 ✅
Agar kisi ne pehle se +919876543210 ya 919876543210 daal rakha hai — woh bhi sahi kaam karega
Form mein hint bhi likh diya: "WhatsApp (10 digit, bina +91)"
CSV import mein bhi: Same rule — 10 digit daalein, app handle kar lega.
